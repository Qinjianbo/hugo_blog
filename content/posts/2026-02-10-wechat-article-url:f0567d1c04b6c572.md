## 正文

<p><span leaf="">你可能在很多地方听过一句话：高风险高收益。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">很多人就这么信了，觉得只要敢冒险，就能赚更多。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">但真的是这样吗？</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">今天我们就从最基础的无风险利率说起，一步步把投资收益的逻辑讲清楚。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="color: rgb(47, 118, 195);font-size: 20px;font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">一、先搞懂：什么是无风险利率</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">可以这么理解，</span><span style="text-decoration: underline;color: rgb(250, 83, 89);font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">无风险利率就是整个市场的收益底线。</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">在咱们国内，大家普遍用10年期国债收益率作为这个基准。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">截至2026年2月10日，这个利率大概是 1.80%。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">为什么是它？</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">因为国债背后是国家信用，几乎不存在还不上钱的可能，</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">安全、确定、不用担惊受怕。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">它就是一把尺子，所有其他投资的收益，都是在它的基础上往上加。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="font-weight: bold;font-size: 20px;color: rgb(47, 118, 195);"><span leaf="">二、收益金字塔：每多赚一点，多承担了什么？</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">我们以1.80%为底座，从低到高排一层投资金字塔，你就能看明白，不同资产之间的收益差异到底来自哪里。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="color: rgb(248, 173, 25);font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">1. 10年期国债：1.80%</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">完全无风险，保本保息，收益就是底线。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="color: rgb(248, 173, 25);font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">2. 国开债：1.92%</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">安全性接近国债，只是发行主体不同，多出来一点点收益，算是微小的风险补偿。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="color: rgb(248, 173, 25);font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">3. AAA央企债：2.10%左右</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">大型央企发行，经营风险极低，收益再高一点。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="color: rgb(248, 173, 25);font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">4. AA+国企/城投债：2.50%左右</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">地方国企、平台债，不再是绝对刚性保障，风险再上一级。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="color: rgb(248, 173, 25);font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">5. 公募REITs：3.0%左右</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">相当于投资物业、高速等资产，有租金波动和价格波动。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="font-weight: bold;color: rgb(248, 173, 25);"><span leaf="">6. 高股息蓝筹股：6.3%左右</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">大盘龙头公司股票，股价会涨跌，需要承受市场波动。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="font-weight: bold;color: rgb(248, 173, 25);"><span leaf="">7. 小盘成长股：7.8%左右</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">公司经营不确定性更强，股价波动更大。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="color: rgb(248, 173, 25);font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">8. 私募股权：12%以上</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">资金一锁好几年，企业甚至可能倒闭，风险很高。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="color: rgb(248, 173, 25);font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">9. 期货、期权、杠杆产品</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">收益不封顶，亏损也不封顶，属于纯博弈。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">你会发现一个规律：</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="text-decoration: underline;color: rgb(250, 83, 89);font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">收益越高，要承担的不确定性、波动、流动性限制就越多。</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="color: rgb(250, 83, 89);font-weight: bold;text-decoration: underline;"><span leaf="">收益之间的差距，本质就是风险溢价的差异。</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="color: rgb(47, 118, /></span><span style="text-decoration: underline;color: rgb(250, 83, 89);font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">高风险不代表一定给你高收益，只代表结果更不确定。</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br leaf="">你愿意冒更大的亏本金风险，才有机会拿到更高收益，</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">而不是一定能拿到。</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">比如你投100万去做预期12%的私募，</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">最后可能赚12%，也可能亏30%，甚至亏光。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">风险高，只是盈亏的空间都变大了，不是稳赚。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="font-size: 20px;font-weight: bold;color: rgb(47, 118, 195);"><span leaf="">四、简单总结</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">无风险利率是所有投资的定价锚，</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf="">各类资产的收益差距，都来自风险、流动性、确定性的差别。</span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="text-decoration: underline;color: rgb(250, 83, 89);font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">高风险对应的是更大的不确定性，</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="text-decoration: underline;color: rgb(250, 83, 89);font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">而不是稳稳的高收益。</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="text-decoration: underline;color: rgb(250, 83, 89);font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">理性的投资，从来不是越冒险越好，</span></span><span leaf=""><br  /></span><span style="text-decoration: underline;color: rgb(250, 83, 89);font-weight: bold;"><span leaf="">而是只赚自己看得懂、承受得起的钱。</span></span><span
